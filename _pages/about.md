I'm a PhD student within Nick Talbot's lab at The Sainsbury Laboratory, Norwich, UK. My research focuses on characterising effector proteins of the blast fungus *Magnaporthe oryze*.

Previously, I studied Biological Sciences at the University of Birmingham (UK) where I graduated with an integrated Master's in Science (MSci). I completed my final year research project within Megan McDonald's lab, focusing on the large transposon *Sanctuary I* within the fungal phytopathogen *Bipolaris sorokiniana*.
