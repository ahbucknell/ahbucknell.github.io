Summary

PhD candidate in molecular plant-microbe interactions at The Sainsbury Laboratory, using novel protein design and structural bioinformatics to decode effector function in the fungal blast pathogen Magnaporthe oryzae. Background in fungal transposon biology (Starships) and horizontal gene transfer, with 4 publications and preprints. Active in public engagement and science communication.
